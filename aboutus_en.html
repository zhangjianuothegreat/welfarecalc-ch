About Us — WelfareCalc.ch

Welcome to WelfareCalc.ch. We are an independent, purely function-oriented Swiss platform that helps citizens carry out complex legal and financial calculations quickly, precisely, and accessibly. Our offering includes specialized calculators such as the DENTOTAR dental bill checker, lease agreement reviews, and precise simulations for cantonal social benefits.

Algorithm Transparency & Official Data Sources

The calculations on WelfareCalc.ch are strictly based on the official legal requirements of the federal government and the individual implementing provisions of the 26 Swiss cantons (e.g., for the priority per-capita deductible logic of individual premium reductions or the regional imputed rental value limits for supplementary benefits)[cite: 3, 5].

To ensure maximum integrity, we not only refrain from any background data collection but also disclose the calculation logic: over 1,400 transparent algorithm disclosures have been implemented for all cantons[cite: 3, 5]. This makes every calculation step 100% mathematically and legally traceable for you, authorities, or subject matter experts.

Uncompromising Privacy („Privacy-First“ Layout)

Important note on data protection: All calculations on this website are performed entirely locally in your web browser. None of the financial, health, or personal data you enter is transmitted to our servers, stored there, or evaluated[cite: 3]. Your privacy remains absolutely untouched.

Why we do this

Accessing state funding, checking rental rates, or understanding a dental bill according to the DENTOTAR system often requires in-depth expertise[cite: 3, 5]. Our goal is to lower this barrier. We provide you with a clear, ad-free, and privacy-compliant tool that serves as a reliable orientation aid in everyday Swiss legal and administrative matters.

Who we are

Behind WelfareCalc.ch there is neither a large corporation nor a government agency, but an independent Swiss development project dedicated to digital transparency and consumer protection[cite: 2, 3]. We believe that important calculation tools should be freely accessible to everyone, mathematically precise, and usable without the risk of data misuse[cite: 3].
