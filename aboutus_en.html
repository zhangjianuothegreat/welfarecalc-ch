About Us — WelfareCalc.ch

Welcome to WelfareCalc.ch. We are an independent, purely function-driven Swiss civic-tech platform with a clear mission: we help citizens perform complex official calculations and bill reviews quickly, accurately, and accessibly – whether it is cantonal social benefit simulations, DENTOTAR® dental bill verification, or rental contract compliance checks.

Switzerland is complex – especially regarding social benefits, health costs and tenancy law

Switzerland is a small country, but it has 26 cantons – and each canton has its own highly complex rules for social benefits. These rules also change regularly. This extremely fragmented system not only overwhelms recent arrivals, but also causes information asymmetries and uncertainties even for native Swiss citizens.

But it is not only social welfare that is complicated. In everyday life, we also encounter highly specialised administrative hurdles:

The DENTOTAR® billing system for dental treatments: Invoice items are often opaque. Laypersons can hardly verify whether the billing is correct or whether the tax point values comply with official specifications.

Complex tenancy law: Reference interest rate, ancillary costs and regulations regarding rent deposits – tenants are often at a disadvantage due to information deficits.

Official documents are full of technical terms and difficult-to-understand paragraphs. This is exactly where we step in to remove these barriers.

Our story: from personal hurdles to a global civic-tech project

The idea for this project arose from a very personal experience: our founder and lead developer lived in Switzerland and was confronted with the bureaucratic hurdles of the federalist system with its 26 different cantonal regulations. Out of frustration with these information imbalances grew the determination to create a tool that translates complex guidelines, billing logic and legal texts into clear, understandable language.

After his professional return to Hong Kong in 2021, this idea did not let him go. As an experienced network engineer, he used his free time to lay the mathematical foundation for WelfareCalc. Soon, it developed into a passionate cross-border initiative: today, the project is supported by a small, dedicated core team consisting of Swiss expats living in Hong Kong, our lead developer, and volunteers directly from Switzerland (including two software engineers with a background in Swiss financial systems and a volunteer with a paralegal focus on tenancy law).

Since Switzerland is multilingual and many people with different native languages live here, we offer our content in 11 languages. Language should not be a barrier when it comes to access to important everyday information. To manage this immense editorial and technical work, we rely on the power of the community as a pure open-source project.

Algorithm transparency & official data sources

The calculation logic and the disclosed algorithms on WelfareCalc.ch are strictly based on the publicly available information from the Federal Social Insurance Office (FSIO), the legal texts of the 26 cantonal authorities, and the official DENTOTAR® tariff of Swiss dentists (as of 2026).

To ensure maximum integrity, we avoid any black-box systems and fully disclose the calculation logic: more than 1,400 transparent algorithm disclosures have been implemented for all cantons. Each calculation step is thus mathematically and legally fully traceable for you, authorities or experts. (Note: our source code for the calculation logic is released for public review and independent auditing.)

Uncompromising privacy („Privacy-First“ approach)

Important privacy notice (revDSG-compliant):
All calculations on this website are performed entirely locally in your web browser. None of the financial, health, rental or personal data you enter is transmitted to our servers, stored there, or used for analytical purposes. Your privacy remains completely untouched. Processing happens transiently in the working memory of your own device.

Independent infrastructure & transparency

Behind WelfareCalc.ch there is no large corporation and no state authority, but an independent, entirely self-financed digital project committed to transparency and consumer protection in Switzerland. We believe that important calculation tools should be freely accessible to everyone, mathematically precise, and usable without the risk of data misuse.

For reasons of transparency, we also disclose our technical structure: our domain was deliberately acquired through the leading Swiss registrar Hostpoint. At the same time, to guarantee maximum reliability, uncompromising data protection and lightning-fast loading times for users within Switzerland, we use the global Cloudflare network (edge computing) for the secure delivery of our open-source scripts.

Since the Swiss system is huge and highly federalist, we check all algorithms with the greatest care against the official cantonal calculators. Should you nevertheless encounter discrepancies or outdated cantonal tariffs, we would appreciate your help to make this offer more reliable for everyone.
