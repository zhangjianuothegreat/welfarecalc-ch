About Us — WelfareCalc.ch

Welcome to WelfareCalc.ch. We are an independent Swiss civic-tech platform with a clear mission: we help you perform complex official calculations and invoice verifications quickly, accurately, and effortlessly – whether for cantonal social benefit simulations, DENTOTAR dental invoice audits, or rental contract compliance checks.

Switzerland is complex – especially when it comes to social benefits, healthcare costs, and tenancy law

Switzerland is a small country, but it has 26 cantons – and each canton has its own rules for social benefits. These rules change regularly. This highly fragmented system not only overwhelms newcomers but also causes confusion even among native Swiss citizens.

But social welfare is not the only complicated area. In everyday life, we encounter highly specialised information gaps:

  • The DENTOTAR billing system for dental treatments: Invoices are often opaque. Laypeople can hardly verify whether the bill is correct or whether the tariff point values comply with official guidelines.
  • Complex tenancy law: Reference interest rates, ancillary costs, and rent deposit regulations – tenants are often at a disadvantage due to significant information asymmetries.

Official documents are full of technical jargon and hard-to-understand paragraphs. Anyone who wants to know and enforce their rights often faces tedious detective work.

This is exactly where we step in.

Our goal: Translating complicated rules into plain language

The idea for this project arose from a simple thought: reduce information asymmetries – and translate complicated guidelines, billing logic, and legal texts into clear, understandable language.

We want to explain the complex rules of cantonal social benefits, DENTOTAR logic, and the most important clauses in tenancy law as simply as possible – so that you truly understand your rights and obligations.

Since Switzerland is multilingual and many people with different native languages live here, we offer our content in 11 languages. Language should not be a barrier when it comes to accessing important information.

We do not replace authorities and do not provide legally binding decisions. We see ourselves as a digital key that makes it easier for you to access an often opaque set of rules. In the long term, we aim to turn WelfareCalc into a kind of "Wikipedia" for the Swiss social, health, and tenancy law landscape – open, neutral, and usable by everyone.

Our team: Not a corporation, but a dedicated circle of volunteers

Behind WelfareCalc is neither a large corporation nor a government agency, but a small, non-profit team of volunteers. We maintain this platform in our free time and bring diverse backgrounds together:

  • 🌐 Network engineers responsible for system architecture and data protection
  • 🏛️ Swiss administrative staff who know the social system intimately
  • ⚖️ Law students from the University of Zurich
  • 🗣️ Multilingual volunteers handling translations and localisations

Every algorithm release – whether for social benefit calculation, DENTOTAR auditing, or rental contract checking – is internally reviewed by us.

But let’s be honest: we make mistakes too. The Swiss system is vast and complex. We cannot guarantee that all data and logic are 100% error-free. That is why we place the highest value on transparency and welcome any form of public scrutiny.

Transparent algorithms and local computation – your privacy comes first

All calculations on WelfareCalc.ch are strictly based on federal legal requirements as well as cantonal implementing provisions. To ensure you can feel completely secure, we follow two core principles:

100% transparency of our algorithms: We do not work with "black boxes". For all cantons and application areas, we have implemented over 1,400 transparent algorithm disclosures. Every calculation step is mathematically and legally fully traceable.

No data collection (local browser computation): Your privacy belongs to you. All calculations and checks take place exclusively in your local browser. The data you enter – whether your income, sensitive financial information, or invoice details – is never transmitted to our servers, stored there, or used for analytics.
