About Us — WelfareCalc.ch

Welcome to WelfareCalc.ch. We are an independent, purely functional Swiss civic-tech platform with a clear mission: we help citizens perform complex official calculations and invoice verifications quickly, accurately, and effortlessly – whether for cantonal social benefit simulations, DENTOTAR® dental invoice audits, or rental contract compliance checks.

Switzerland is complex – especially when it comes to social benefits, healthcare costs, and tenancy law

Switzerland is a small country, but it has 26 cantons – and each canton has its own highly complex rules for social benefits. These rules change regularly. This fragmented system not only overwhelms newcomers but also causes significant information asymmetries and confusion even among native Swiss citizens.

But social welfare is not the only complicated area. In everyday life, we encounter specialized administrative hurdles and information gaps:

The DENTOTAR® billing system for dental treatments: Invoices are often opaque. Laypeople can hardly verify whether the breakdown is correct or whether the tariff point values comply with official guidelines.

Complex tenancy law: Reference interest rates, ancillary costs, and rent deposit regulations – tenants are frequently at a disadvantage due to information deficits.

Official documents are full of technical jargon and hard-to-understand statutory paragraphs. This is exactly where we step in to bridge these gaps.

Our goal: Translating complicated rules into plain language

The idea for this project arose from a simple thought: reduce information asymmetries – and translate complicated guidelines, billing logic, and legal texts into clear, understandable language.

We want to explain the complex rules of cantonal social benefits, DENTOTAR® logic, and the most important clauses in tenancy law as simply as possible – so that you truly understand your rights and obligations.

Since Switzerland is multilingual and many people with different native languages live here, we offer our content in 11 languages. Language should not be a barrier when it comes to accessing vital everyday information.

We do not replace authorities and do not provide legally binding decisions. We see ourselves as a digital key that makes it easier for you to access an often opaque set of rules. In the long term, we aim to turn WelfareCalc into a kind of open knowledge platform for the Swiss social, health, and tenancy law landscape – neutral, open, and usable by everyone.

Algorithm Transparency & Official Data Sources

The calculation logic and the disclosed algorithms on WelfareCalc.ch are strictly based on publicly available data from the Federal Office of Social Insurance (FOSI / BSV), statutory texts from the 26 cantonal authorities, and the official DENTOTAR® tariff guidelines established for Swiss dentists (as of 2026).

To ensure maximum integrity, we do not employ any black-box systems and completely disclose our calculation logic: over 1,400 transparent algorithm disclosures have been implemented across all cantons. Every calculation step is thus mathematically and legally fully traceable for you, authorities, or expert auditors. (Note: Our source code for the calculation logic is available for public verification and independent auditing.)

Uncompromising Privacy („Privacy-First“ Layout)

Important Privacy Notice (revDSG-compliant):
All calculations on this website take place entirely locally within your web browser. None of the financial, health, rental, or personal data you enter is ever transmitted to our servers, stored there, or processed for analytics. Your privacy remains absolutely untouched. All processing runs transiently within the volatile memory (RAM) of your own device.

Who we are: An independent civic-tech project

Behind WelfareCalc.ch is neither a large corporation nor a government agency, but an independent, entirely self-funded digital project dedicated to transparency and consumer protection in Switzerland. We believe that critical calculation tools should be freely accessible to everyone, mathematically precise, and safely usable without the risk of data exploitation.

As the Swiss system is vast and highly federalized, we review all algorithms with the utmost care against official cantonal calculators. Should you nevertheless encounter discrepancies or outdated cantonal tariffs, we highly welcome your feedback to help make this service more reliable for everyone.
