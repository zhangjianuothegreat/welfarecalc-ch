Swiss Social Benefits Calculator – Free & Anonymous

Our tool helps you quickly determine your eligibility for AHV, IV, supplementary benefits, and social assistance. Select your situation above to start the personalized calculation.

Which benefits are calculated?

The WelfareCalc.ch calculator covers the following Swiss social benefits (calculation based on the latest guidelines, as of 2026):

  • AHV/IV – Old-age and Survivors' Insurance / Disability Insurance
  • Supplementary Benefits (EL) – for AHV/IV recipients with low income
  • Unemployment Compensation – unemployment benefits and short-time work compensation
  • Premium Reduction (IPV) – health insurance premium reductions
  • Social Assistance – cantonal and municipal support
  • Scholarships & Training Contributions – for students and vocational training
  • Family Allowances – child and education allowances

Who is the calculator suitable for?

The calculator is optimized for the following groups: individuals, families, unemployed persons, students, retirees, low-income individuals, persons with disabilities, protection seekers, and pregnant women.

Free, anonymous and privacy-compliant

Using WelfareCalc.ch is completely free and requires no registration. No personal data is stored. Your inputs remain anonymous and are not shared with third parties.
