About WelfareCalc.ch

WelfareCalc.ch is a private, independent, and non-commercial information portal for individuals in Switzerland. The tools offered include:

  • Social benefits calculator (AHV, IV, EL, IPV, social welfare, family allowances, etc.)
  • Swiss Dental Bill Auditor (DENTOTAR® invoice verification)
  • Swiss Rental Protector (lease agreement scan for over 30 potential pitfalls)
