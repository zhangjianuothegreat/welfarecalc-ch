About WelfareCalc.ch

WelfareCalc.ch is a private, independent, non-commercial information and open data portal for individuals in Switzerland. The digital tools and directories offered include:

  • Social benefits calculators (AHV, IV, Supplementary benefits EL, Individual premium reduction IPV, social assistance, family allowances, etc.)
  • Swiss Dental Bill Auditor (DENTOTAR® invoice verification)
  • Mietvertrag-Schutz / Swiss Rental Protector (lease agreement scan for over 30 pitfalls)
  • Open Algorithms and Documentation Directory: A transparent disclosure of over 1,400 pages of detailed calculation logic and cantonal legislative formulas for all 26 Swiss cantons.
