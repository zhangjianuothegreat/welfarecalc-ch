3. Purpose, Legal Basis, and Transparency

The processing of purely technical data is carried out to provide website functionality, in particular the Social Benefits Calculator, the Swiss Dental Bill Auditor, and the Rental Contract Protector (Swiss Rental Protector)[cite: 3, 5]. The legal basis is Art. 31 of the revised Swiss Federal Data Protection Act (revDSG) – legitimate interest in the functionality and security of the website.

No automated individual decision-making or profiling: Although the platform performs complex calculations based on more than 1,400 documented cantonal and federal legal algorithms, there is **no automated decision-making** within the meaning of revDSG[cite: 3, 5]. The algorithms serve purely simulation purposes to increase consumer transparency; no user profiles are created nor legal obligations established.

Important note: All tools are for informational and transparency purposes only. They do not constitute legal, medical, or dental advice. We accept no liability for decisions made based on these calculations. For rental agreements, we always recommend a review by a tenant association or qualified attorney.

4. Retention Period

Server log files (technical access data) are stored for a maximum of 7 to 30 days and then automatically deleted. All calculation and scan data (social benefits, DENTOTAR codes, rental agreement content) remain exclusively and temporarily in your browser‘s local memory and are neither transmitted to us nor stored permanently[cite: 3].

We only disclose your data to third parties to the extent strictly necessary for the technical delivery of the website. This website is hosted and delivered via Cloudflare Inc. (USA). Cloudflare processes the aforementioned technical data (log files) to ensure stability, DDoS protection, and fast loading times within Switzerland. Appropriate standard contractual clauses for data protection have been implemented.

For privacy-compliant font display, we use Bunny Fonts (a service by Squadra Digital, EU servers). Unlike traditional CDNs, no IP addresses are logged and no user profiles are created, guaranteeing full compliance with revDSG.

5.3 PDF Generation – jsPDF via Cloudflare CDN

The “Download PDF” feature uses the open-source library jsPDF. Data retrieval and document rendering occur 100% locally on your device; the CDN merely provides the static script file.

5.4 No Other Third Parties

We do not integrate any other external services. In particular, we do not use Google Fonts, Google Maps, YouTube, social media plugins, or advertising networks.
