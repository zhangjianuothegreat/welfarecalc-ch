2. Detailed Calculation Flow

1

Recognised Expenditure (Needs)

Geneva belongs to Region 1 (urban centres), which applies the highest rent ceilings in Switzerland:

Basic needs + Gross rent (up to limit) + Average health insurance premium

2

Countable Income

Your income is assessed according to SPC Geneva EL rules:

  • Pensions: AHV or IV pensions (excluding the 13th AHV pension).
  • Asset depletion: (Net assets - allowance) / 10 for OASI, or / 15 for DI.
  • Earned income: 2/3 of income after deducting the lump-sum allowance (CHF 1,300 single / CHF 1,950 couples).

3

EL Result

The difference gives your monthly entitlement:

Annual EL = [Total recognised expenditure] - [Countable income]
Important: Asset threshold (access condition)
In Geneva, EL is payable only if net assets (excluding owner-occupied housing value) are below CHF 100,000 (single persons) or CHF 200,000 (married couples).
