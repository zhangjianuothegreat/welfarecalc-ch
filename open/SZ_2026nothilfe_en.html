1. Legal Bases & Guidelines

The granting of emergency aid in the Canton of Schwyz is based on Art. 12 of the Federal Constitution (BV) as well as the cantonal legislation of the Cantonal Council of Schwyz. In contrast to regular asylum social assistance, emergency aid only covers the absolute minimum subsistence level.

⚠️ Important legislative change from April 2026:
According to the Migration Act revised by the Cantonal Council of Schwyz (introduction of cashless plastic money for asylum seekers and those ordered to leave), the calculated emergency aid credits will generally no longer be paid out in cash. The amounts will be fully credited to an electronic payment card (debit card). Cash payments are limited to minimal exceptions (e.g., bus tickets).

2. System Parameters 2026 (Collective Accommodation)

Since rejected asylum seekers in the Canton of Schwyz are housed in cantonal collective accommodations (e.g., emergency shelter Kaltbach), benefits in kind for lodging and infrastructure take precedence. The daily rates for personal needs are set as follows:

Base Daily Rate (Single Person)

CHF 8.00 / Day

Couple Factor (couple_factor)

1.68 (CHF 13.44 for Couples)

Child Supplement (Children 1 & 2)

CHF 4.16 / Day (Factor 0.52)

Degression from the 3rd Child

Factor 0.10 (Bonus Factor)

3. The Mathematical Calculation Algorithm

The determination of the monthly emergency aid entitlement is carried out via a three-stage mathematical loop:

1
Calculation of the Adult Base Amount ($A_{daily}$)

Depending on the household structure (single person or married couple/cohabitation), the degressive factor is applied:

If adults == 1: A_daily = 8.00
If adults >= 2: A_daily = 8.00 * 1.68 + (adults - 2) * 8.00
2
Calculation of the Child Supplement ($C_{daily}$)

For the first two children, the regular supplement (factor 0.52) is calculated. From the 3rd child onwards, a degressive reduction applies, where a bonus factor of 0.10 is added for each additional child:

If children <= 2: C_daily = children * (8.00 * 0.52)
If children > 2: C_daily = 2 * (8.00 * 0.52) + (children - 2) * (8.00 * 0.10)
3
Time Projection and Commercial Rounding

Emergency aid in the Canton of Schwyz is granted on a daily basis. For standard monthly planning in the system, the exact average value of 30.42 days per month is used and rounded commercially.

Total_Month_Raw = (A_daily + C_daily) * 30.42
Final_Month = Math.round(Total_Month_Raw)
