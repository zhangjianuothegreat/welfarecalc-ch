2

Assessable Income

All income is evaluated according to the 2026 EL reform rules:

  • 13th AHV pension: This remains neutral in the EL calculation (neutralization).
  • Pension receipt: Inclusion of monthly AHV/IV pensions (12 months).
  • Asset depletion: (Net assets - exemption amount) / 10 (for old-age pensioners) or 1/15 (for IV).
  • Earned income: 2/3 of income after statutory deduction is counted.

3

Result: EL Entitlement

The difference yields your annual entitlement:

Annual EL = [Sum of Expenses] - [Sum of Income]

The amount is divided by 12 and paid out monthly.

Asset threshold (Entry threshold)
A legal entitlement to supplementary benefits exists only if the net assets (excluding owner-occupied property) are below CHF 100'000 (single persons) or CHF 200'000 (married couples).
