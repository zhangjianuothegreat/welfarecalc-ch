Trust is built through transparency. Here we disclose the mathematical logic used by our system to calculate your premium reduction entitlement in the Canton of Bern. This algorithm is based on the official step model of the Social Insurance Office (ASV).

1. Core Parameters (2026)

Asset Assessment Rate

5.0 %

5% of the adjusted net assets are added to the income.

Asset Allowance

CHF 17,000

This amount is deducted from net assets per person.

Earned Income Deduction

Max. CHF 3,000

5% of earned income, capped at CHF 3,000.

2. Calculation Steps

1

Relevant Income (LNA)

The LNA determines your classification:

LNA = Taxable Income + (Adj. Assets × 5%) - Social Deductions - Earned Income Deduction

Each additional child + CHF 10,000
