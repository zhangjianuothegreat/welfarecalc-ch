In the canton of Basel-Landschaft (BL), family allowances are governed by the federal Family Allowances Act (FamZG). Our calculator uses the official cantonal parameters for the year 2026 to ensure a complete and transparent verification of your entitlement.

1. Monthly Rates BL (2026)

Child Allowance

CHF 215 / month

From the month of birth until the end of the 16th year of age.

Education Allowance

CHF 268 / month

From age 16 (or from age 15 for post-compulsory education) up to max. age 25.

2. Calculation Methodology

1

Category Determination & Age Limits

The algorithm checks the age, employment status, and educational situation of each child individually:

  • Children under 16 years: General entitlement to child allowance (CHF 215).
  • Youth in post-compulsory education (from age 15): Early entitlement to education allowance (CHF 268) possible.
  • Youth aged 16 to 25 years: Entitlement to education allowance, provided certified education or studies are underway.
  • Children unable to work due to disability: Extended entitlement to child allowance (CHF 215) until the end of the 20th year of age.
2

Total Monthly Amount Calculation

The monthly total is based on a purely linear calculation. The Canton of Basel-Landschaft does not provide progressive increases from the third child onward:

Monthly Entitlement = (Number of Child Allowances × CHF 215) + (Number of Education Allowances × CHF 268)

*Important: According to FamZG Art. 3, only one type of allowance can be claimed per child at any given time (exclusion principle).

3

One-Time Allowances (Birth/Adoption)

According to cantonal decree, the Canton of Basel-Landschaft grants no one-time birth or adoption allowances.

Allowance Type Amount (BL)
Birth Allowance CHF 0
Adoption Allowance CHF 0
4

Income Limits & Annual Entitlement

The algorithm multiplies the validated monthly amount by the number of months in the current calendar year:

Annual Amount = (Monthly Total Entitlement) × 12

Income Limitation (Background Validation): If a child in education earns an AHV-taxable income exceeding CHF 2,520 / month (CHF 30,240 / year), the entitlement to education allowance is completely revoked.

Priority Order According to Federal Law:
If both parents work, the algorithm prevents double claims by strictly adhering to the statutory priority order according to FamZG Art. 12 (place of benefit claim, primary and secondary employment).
