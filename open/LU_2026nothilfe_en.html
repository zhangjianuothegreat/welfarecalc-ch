Transparency builds trust. In the Canton of Lucerne (LU), emergency aid for people in need is calculated using a precise mathematical model. Here we disclose the official parameters and the calculation algorithm for the year 2026.

1. Calculation Parameters (Lucerne 2026)

Basic daily rate (single rate)

CHF 12.80 / Day

Basic needs for a single person (one of the highest rates in Switzerland).

Couple factor (degression)

Factor 1.75

Two adults in a household receive 175% of the basic rate.

Child supplement

Factor 0.58 / 0.15

58% of basic rate per child + 15% bonus supplement from the 3rd child onward.

2. Calculation Process (Step by Step)

1

Determine daily rate for adults

The daily rate for adults is based on the number of people in the assistance household:

Single person: CHF 12.80 / day
Couple (2 adults): CHF 12.80 + (1 × 12.80 × 1.75) = CHF 35.20 / day

Note: For more than 2 adults, each additional person is added according to the cantonal key with the degressive rate.

2

Child supplement & large family bonus

Lucerne grants a basic rate for children and an additional bonus from the 3rd child onward:

Basic child rate: CHF 12.80 × 0.58 = CHF 7.42 / day
Bonus (from 3rd child): CHF 12.80 × 0.15 = CHF 1.92 / day additional
3

Monthly extrapolation & rounding

The daily entitlements are extrapolated to the statistical month (30.42 days) and rounded commercially.

Monthly entitlement = ROUND( (Total daily amount × 30.42) ; 0 )
Special features in Lucerne:
• Accommodation form: The rate of CHF 12.80 primarily applies to "mixed housing" concepts.
• Education: Children in education are considered at the child rate until graduation (max. 25 years).
• Subsidiarity: Own assets or income must be prioritized.
