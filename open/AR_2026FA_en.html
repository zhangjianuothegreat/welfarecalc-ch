In the Canton of Appenzell Ausserrhoden (AR), family benefits are based on federal law (FamZG) and cantonal implementing provisions. Our algorithm calculates your entitlement precisely based on the 2026 rates.

1. Monthly Rates AR (2026)

Child Allowance

CHF 230 / month

For children from the month of birth up to the age of 16.

Education Allowance

CHF 290 / month

From age 16 (or age 15 with proven education) up to max. 25 years.

2. Calculation Methodology

1

Determining the Category

The algorithm checks for each child the eligibility criteria:

  • 0 - 16 years: Entitled to child allowance (CHF 230).
  • 16 - 25 years: Entitled to education allowance (CHF 290), provided an approved education or training program is being pursued.
  • Children with disabilities: Child allowance is granted up to age 20.
2

Calculating the Monthly Total Amount

In the Canton of Appenzell Ausserrhoden, there is no additional supplement for large families (no extra allowance starting from the 3rd child):

(# of children × CHF 230) + (# in education × CHF 290)

*Important: The algorithm allocates only one type of allowance per child (no double counting).

3

One-off Allowances (Birth / Adoption)

The Canton of Appenzell Ausserrhoden provides no statutory birth or adoption allowances.

Type of AllowanceAmount (AR)
Birth allowanceCHF 0
Adoption allowanceCHF 0
4

Determining the Annual Entitlement

Payments are usually made monthly by the employer or directly via the compensation office (e.g., for self-employed persons):

Annual amount = (Monthly sum) × 12
Note on Priority Rules:
If both parents are employed, Art. 12 FamZG regulates the entitlement order. Our algorithm assumes the primary entitled person.
