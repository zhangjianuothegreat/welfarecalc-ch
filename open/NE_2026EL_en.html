2

Countable Income

Special feature 2026: The introduction of the 13th AHV pension is treated neutrally in the EL calculation.

  • Pensions: AHV/IV benefits (13th pension disregarded).
  • Asset depletion: (Net assets - exemption) / 10 (old-age pension) or 1/15 (disability insurance).
  • Earned income: 2/3 of income counted after deducting allowances.

3

Final EL Benefit

EL is calculated as a deficit guarantee:

Annual EL = [Sum of recognised expenditure] - [Sum of countable income]
Important: Asset threshold
Entitlement exists only if net assets (excluding owner-occupied property) are below CHF 100'000 (single persons) or CHF 200'000 (married couples).
