2

Assessable income

Here, all income is assessed according to EL rules:

  • Pension income: AHV/IV pensions (excluding the 13th AHV pension, which remains neutral in EL calculation).
  • Asset depletion: (Net assets - allowance) / 10 (for old-age pension) or 1/15 (for disability insurance).
  • Earned income: Only 2/3 of income is counted after deducting the lump-sum allowance.

3

Result: EL entitlement

The difference gives your monthly or annual entitlement:

EL benefit = [Total expenditure] - [Total income]
Important: The eligibility threshold
As of 2021, the asset threshold applies: If you have net assets exceeding CHF 100,000 (single persons) or CHF 200,000 (married couples), you have no legal entitlement to EL (owner-occupied property value excluded).
