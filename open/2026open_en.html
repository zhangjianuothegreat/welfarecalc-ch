🇨🇭 About welfarecalc.ch

We are a team of engineers and developers based in Switzerland. Our goal is to make the complex Swiss social benefit systems understandable and accessible to everyone. This platform is a non-profit project born out of a commitment to transparency and fairness.

📄 How our algorithms are created
Our calculation logic is based exclusively on official, publicly available documents – including cantonal laws (SHG, ELG, FamZG, KVG), SKOS guidelines, and ordinances from compensation funds. Each algorithm is carefully derived from these sources and transparently documented.

🌍 Multilingualism & transparency
Switzerland is diverse. That's why we provide all algorithms in 11 languages: German, French, Italian, English, Spanish, Portuguese, Polish, Croatian, Albanian, Turkish, and Tamil. This ensures that as many people as possible can understand the information relevant to them.

📌 How to use this page
Select your canton and the desired benefit type above. Then click "Search & open algorithm". You will be redirected directly to the corresponding algorithm – in a new tab or the same window (depending on your browser settings). The red button "Back to homepage" will take you back to the main welfarecalc.ch page at any time.
