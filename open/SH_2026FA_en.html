In Switzerland, family allowances are regulated by federal law (FamZG). Our calculator uses the specific cantonal rates for the Canton of Schaffhausen for 2026 to accurately determine your entitlement. Transparency is important to us.

1. Monthly Rates (2026)

Child Allowance

CHF 230 / month

For children from age 0 up to the completion of their 16th year (or until the start of post-compulsory education).

Education Allowance

CHF 290 / month

From the start of post-compulsory education (as early as age 15) up to a maximum of age 25.

2. Calculation Process

1

Determination of Category & Age Limits

The algorithm checks the age and education status of each child:

  • 0 - 16 years: Basic entitlement to child allowance (CHF 230).
  • As early as age 15: If the child has already completed compulsory schooling and is undertaking post-compulsory education, entitlement to the education allowance (CHF 290) already applies.
  • 16 - 25 years: Full entitlement to education allowance (CHF 290) upon proof of enrollment in education/training.
  • Children unable to work: Entitlement to child allowance (CHF 230) until the completion of their 20th year.
2

Apprentice Income Check (Defensive Validation)

The algorithm filters out young people in education who earn too much personal income:

  • If the child's annual earned income from employment exceeds the statutory maximum of CHF 30,240 (equivalent to one full annual AHV old-age pension), entitlement to the education allowance is completely voided.
3

Calculation of the Monthly Amount

The total is formed linearly from the individual rates without progressive scaling:

(Number of children × CHF 230) + (Number in education × CHF 290)

*Important: In the Canton of Schaffhausen, there is no increase for large families (from the 3rd child onward) and no age-dependent scaling within the categories.

4

Lump-sum Allowances (Birth/Adoption)

According to the Cantonal Act on Family and Social Allowances (FSG), the Canton of Schaffhausen grants no one-time birth or adoption allowances.

Type of Allowance Amount (SH)
Birth Allowance CHF 0
Adoption Allowance CHF 0
5

Determination of Annual Entitlement

Payment is made monthly via the employer or directly through the competent family compensation fund:

Annual Amount = (Total Monthly Entitlement) × 12
Entitlement & Priority Rules:
Only one parent may receive the allowance for the same child. If both parents are employed or work in different cantons, the statutory priority rules under Art. 12 FamZG strictly determine the primary entitlement.
