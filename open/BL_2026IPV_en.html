Trust through transparency. Here we disclose in detail how our calculator determines your potential premium reduction (IPV) in the canton of Basel-Landschaft. This mathematical algorithm strictly follows the official legal provisions of the SVA Basel-Landschaft.

1. Core Parameters (2026)

Income Deduction Rate

7.75 %

Proportion of the relevant income deemed affordable for health insurance.

Asset Consumption

10.0 %

Share of taxable assets added to net income.

2. Calculation Process

1

Relevant Income (LNA)

First, we determine the economic capacity of all household members:

LNA = [Annual net income] + ([Taxable assets] × 10%) - [Paid alimony]

Note for 2026: In the canton of Basel-Landschaft, no separate child deduction on the income level is granted starting this year. Relief for families is provided directly through reduced standard premiums for children.

2

Standard Premiums 2026

The canton of Basel-Landschaft defines standardised insurance costs for different age groups each year:

Category Standard premium (per year) Standard premium (per month)
Adults (26 years and older) CHF 4'596 CHF 383
Young adults (18–25 years) CHF 3'816 CHF 318
Children (0–17 years) CHF 1'968 CHF 164
[Total standard premium] = (No. of adults × 4'596) + (No. of young adults × 3'816) + (No. of children × 1'968)
3

Theoretical Entitlement (Subsidy)

The affordable own contribution (income deduction) is subtracted from the total standard premium:

[Income deduction] = LNA × 7.75%
[Theoretical subsidy] = Max(0, [Total standard premium] - [Income deduction])
4

Final Entitlement & Limitation

The actual annual disbursement is limited by the effective premium paid for basic insurance (OKP/KVG):

[Annual IPV] = Min(Round([Theoretical subsidy]), [Actual annual premium*])

*Important: Only the premium for the statutory basic insurance is considered; private supplementary insurance (VVG) is legally excluded. All amounts are rounded to the nearest Swiss franc.

Asset threshold (Hard Cut-off):
If taxable assets exceed CHF 100'000 for single persons or CHF 200'000 for married couples/two-person households, the legal claim to IPV in canton BL is completely forfeited — regardless of income.
