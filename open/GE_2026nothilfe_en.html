In the Canton of Geneva (GE), emergency aid for persons required to leave Switzerland is coordinated by the Hospice général . To foster trust through transparency, we disclose the complete calculation algorithm for the year 2026.

1. Calculation Parameters (Geneva 2026)

Base Daily Rate (single adult)

CHF 10.00 / day

Basic amount for an adult person in individual accommodation.

Partner Factor (second adult)

Factor 1.78

Degression factor for couples or shared households.

Child Surcharge

CHF 6.00 / day

Equivalent to 60% of the adult base rate per child.

2. Calculation Method (Step by Step)

1

Determine Daily Adult Rate

Geneva uses a progressive factor for the second person in the household:

Number of adults = 1: CHF 10.00
Number of adults > 1: (10.00 + (number of adults - 1) × 10.00 × 0.78)

A couple receives CHF 17.80 per day (degression model).

2

Child Allowances & Large Family Bonus

Each child receives CHF 6.00 per day. From the 3rd child onward, an additional bonus of 10% of the base rate (CHF 1.00) is granted according to the federal basis:

Child daily total = (number of children × 6.00) + (IF children >= 3 THEN (children - 2) × 1.00 ELSE 0)
3

Monthly Extrapolation & Rounding

The daily total is multiplied by the monthly factor 30.42 and rounded commercially to the nearest Swiss franc.

Monthly amount = ROUND( (Total Daily Rate × 30.42) ; 0 )
Important note for Geneva (GE):
• The rates apply to individual accommodation . If accommodated in collective centers with meals, benefits in kind are deducted directly from the cash benefit.
• Legal basis: Loi sur l'insertion et l'aide sociale individuelle (LIASI) & Hospice général guidelines 2026.
