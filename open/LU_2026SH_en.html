Trust through transparency. Here we disclose the full algorithm powering the calculations for the Canton of Lucerne on welfarecalc.ch. The logic is based on the Lucerne Social Assistance Act (SHG LU) and the 2026 SKOS guidelines.

1. Asset Check (The threshold)

Asset allowance per household

Single: CHF 6,000 | Couple: CHF 12,000

Plus CHF 3,000 per child. Total maximum allowance CHF 15,000.

Asset depletion (notional income)

1/10 per year

Excess assets are treated as notional income spread over 10 years.

2. Calculation path (Monthly)

1

Basic living expenses (GBL)

The GBL in the Canton of Lucerne is determined by household composition (2026 rates):

CategoryAmount per month (2026)
Single personCHF 1,061
Couple / 2-person householdCHF 1,624
Additional adult+ CHF 400
Child (11–15 years)CHF 699
Child (under 11 years)CHF 391
2

Housing costs & IPV primacy (individual premium reduction)

Actual rent is considered up to the cantonal maximum:

Recognised rent = Min(Actual rent, Cantonal benchmark)

Health insurance (KVG): In the Canton of Lucerne, IPV benefits (individual premium reduction) are deducted directly from the premium.

Health insurance need = Max(0, Premium - monthly IPV)
3

Earned income & deductions

To encourage employment, an income allowance is granted:

Assessable income = Net wage - 20% deduction (work-related expense allowance)

Additionally, an integration allowance (IZU) of CHF 100 may be granted if the person is employed.

4

Result: Monthly entitlement

The difference calculation determines the monthly assistance amount:

Entitlement = (GBL + Rent + Health insurance remainder + IZU) - (Assessable income + Notional asset depletion)

If the result is negative or zero, no assistance is granted. The social support covers the deficit up to the calculated need.

Note on maximum rent limits:
In the Canton of Lucerne, recognised maximum rent amounts vary by household size (e.g., CHF 1,100 for 1 person, CHF 1,300 for 2 persons). Our algorithm uses the latest cantonal average benchmarks for a fair estimate.
