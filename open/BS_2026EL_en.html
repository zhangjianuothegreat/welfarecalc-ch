Transparency is the foundation of trust in social security systems. Our algorithm for the Canton of Basel-Stadt (BS) strictly follows the 2021/2026 ELG reform and the specific cantonal regulations of the Office for Social Contributions BS.

2. Calculation Process for BS

1

Recognized Expenses (Needs)

Basel-Stadt belongs to Rent Region 1 (centres). The maximum limits are highest here:

2

Countable Income

Special feature for 2026: Neutralization of the 13th AHV pension.

  • Pension Check: AHV/IV pensions are counted only at 12/13, ensuring the new 13th pension does not reduce EL entitlement.
  • Earned Income: Deduction of allowances (CHF 1,400 for singles / CHF 2,100 for couples), then 2/3 of the remaining is counted.
  • Asset depletion: (Assets - allowance) / 10 (for AHV) or 1/15 (for IV).

3

Result: Monthly EL

The difference between your needs and your income gives you the entitlement:

Annual EL Benefit = [Total Needs] - [Total Income]
Important for BS residents:
In the Canton of Basel-Stadt, illness and disability-related costs are reimbursed in addition to the monthly EL benefit, provided they are documented and recognized.
