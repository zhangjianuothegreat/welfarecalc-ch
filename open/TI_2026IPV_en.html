Trust through transparency. Here we disclose how our calculator determines your potential premium reduction in the Canton of Ticino (TI). This algorithm is strictly based on the harmonized guidelines of the cantonal LAPS system.

1. Core Parameters (2026)

Asset Consumption (LAPS)

1/15 (6.67 %)

Portion of taxable assets added to income annually.

Minimum Subsidy

CHF 120 / Year

Amounts below this threshold per person are not paid out.

2. The Calculation Path

1

Available Income (RD) according to LAPS

The LAPS system calculates the adjusted disposable income (Reddito Disponibile) as follows:

RD = ([Net Income] + [Asset Income] + [Taxable Assets / 15]) - ([Social Contributions] + [Alimony] + [Occupational Costs (max. 4'000)] + [Interest Expenses (max. 3'000)])

Note: The calculated RD value is capped at a minimum of CHF 0 if deductions exceed income.

2

Official Reference Premiums (PMR) 2026

The cantonal benchmarks for annual insurance costs in Ticino:

Adults (adult) CHF 8'016 / Year
Young Adults (young_adult, up to 25 in education) CHF 6'143 / Year
Children (child, 0-18) CHF 1'827 / Year
Total PMR = (No. Adults × 8'016) + (No. Young Adults × 6'143) + (No. Children × 1'827)
3

Determination of the Subsidy Rate (λ)

The subsidy rate (λ) is strictly determined in stages based on the calculated RD value:

LAPS Available Income (RD) Subsidy Rate (λ)
RD ≤ CHF 20'000 85 %
CHF 20'000 < RD ≤ CHF 35'000 65 %
CHF 35'000 < RD ≤ CHF 50'000 45 %
CHF 50'000 < RD ≤ CHF 70'000 25 %
CHF 70'000 < RD ≤ CHF 90'000 10 %
RD > CHF 90'000 0 %
4

Entitlement & Federal Minimum Guarantee

The provisional subsidy is calculated from: Total PMR × λ

For families in the middle and lower income range (RD ≤ CHF 50'000) , the statutory federal minimum guarantee for children and young adults also applies:

Category Guaranteed statutory minimum share
Children (0-18) 80 % of the reference premium (CHF 1'461.60)
Young adults in education 50 % of the reference premium (CHF 3'071.50)
If RD ≤ 50'000:
Entitlement = Max([Total PMR × λ], [Sum of Federal Minimum Guarantees])
5

Final Entitlement & Hardship Cap

Annual Entitlement = Min(Rounded Up Entitlement, Actual Health Insurance Premium)

De Minimis Threshold: If the calculated total entitlement is less than CHF 120 per household member per year, the entitlement is forfeited entirely.

Special Feature in the Canton of Ticino:
The Ticino model uses the LAPS model for social protection, which harmoniously combines income and asset components, instead of applying strict asset limits of CHF 100'000 like other cantons.
