2. The Glarus Calculation Method

1

Recognized Expenditure (Needs)

The Canton of Glarus takes into account two rent regions (Region 2 and 3). Our algorithm assigns you based on your municipality of residence:

Basic needs + Rent (actual, up to limit) + Average health insurance premium (KK)

2

Calculable Income

Your income is assessed against the expenditure:

  • Pensions: AHV/IV (excluding the 13th month pension).
  • Asset depletion: (Net assets - allowance) / 10 (old-age pension) or / 15 (disability insurance).
  • Property: For owner-occupied residential property in GL, a special deduction applies.

3

Determination of the EL Benefit

The EL benefit closes the financial gap:

Annual EL benefit = [Total Expenditure] - [Total Income]
Asset threshold (Eligibility threshold)
Since the EL reform of 2021, there is no entitlement if the net assets (excluding property) exceed CHF 100,000 (for single persons) or CHF 200,000 (for married couples).
