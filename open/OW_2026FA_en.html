In Switzerland, family allowances are regulated by law (FamZG). Our calculator uses the specific cantonal rates of Canton Obwalden (OW) for 2026 to precisely determine your entitlement. Transparency is important to us.

1. Monthly Rates OW (2026)

Child Allowance

CHF 220 / month

For children from 0 up to the age of 16.

Education Allowance

CHF 270 / month

From age 16 until the end of education (max. age 25).

2. The Calculation Process

1

Determine the Category

The algorithm checks the age and status of each child:

  • 0 – 16 years: Entitled to child allowance.
  • 16 – 25 years: Entitled to education allowance (proof required).
  • Disabled children: Child allowance until the age of 20.
2

Calculate the Monthly Amount

The total is the sum of the respective individual rates:

(Number of children × CHF 220) + (Number in education × CHF 270)

*Important: In Canton Obwalden, there is no increase for large families (from the 3rd child onward), as is common in other cantons.

3

One-time Allowances (Birth/Adoption)

Unlike some other cantons, Canton Obwalden does not grant any one-time birth or adoption allowances.

Type of Allowance Amount (OW)
Birth allowance CHF 0
Adoption allowance CHF 0
4

Determine the Annual Entitlement

Payment is made monthly via the employer or the family compensation fund:

Annual amount = (Monthly total entitlement) × 12
Eligibility:
Only one parent may receive the allowance. A statutory priority order applies (FamZG Art. 12) if both parents are employed.
