For reasons of transparency and trust, we disclose here the exact mathematical algorithm with which our system determines the statutory emergency aid for the Canton of Valais (VS) in 2026. This calculation is based on Art. 12 of the Federal Constitution and the cantonal implementing provisions for collective accommodation.

1. System Parameters for the Canton of Valais (VS)

Daily Cash Benefit (Base)

CHF 9.25

Basic amount for a single person per day.

Couple Factor (Adults)

1.70

Degression coefficient from the second adult person.

Child Supplement Factor

0.54

Multiplier based on the basic allowance per child.

Note on Child Allowance: In the Canton of Valais, unlike some other cantons, there is no progressive bonus from the 3rd child onwards. Each child is calculated linearly with the factor 0.54.

2. Step-by-Step Calculation Logic

1

Calculation of the Adult Daily Rate

If there is a single adult person in the household, the rate corresponds to the basic amount. For several adults, the couple factor (degression) applies:

Single: AdultDaily = CHF 9.25
Multiple Adults: AdultDaily = 9.25 + (Number of Adults - 1) * 9.25 * 0.70
2

Calculation of the Child Supplement

The supplement for children is summed linearly based on the child allowance factor:

ChildDaily = Number of Children * (9.25 * 0.54)
3

Total Daily Rate and Monthly Projection

The total daily rate results from the sum of the adult and child rates. This value is multiplied by the statistical monthly factor of 30.42 days and rounded commercially:

TotalDaily = AdultDaily + ChildDaily
Monthly Amount = Round(TotalDaily * 30.42)
