In accordance with our philosophy of transparency, we disclose the mathematical basis for the IPV calculator of the Canton of Appenzell Innerrhoden. This algorithm is based on the cantonal guidelines 2026.

1. Core Parameters (2026)

Variable Deductible Rate

7.0 % — 12.0 %

Depending on the relevant income (LNA).

Asset Surcharge

10.0 %

Portion of taxable assets added to income.

2. Calculation Process

1

Relevant Income (LNA)

The LNA forms the basis and is calculated as follows:

[Base Income*] + [10% Taxable Assets]

*Base Income includes: Taxable income, Pillar 3a, Pillar 2 lump-sum payments, and other adjustments according to the guideline.

2

Reference Premiums 2026

The canton of AI sets annual reference values for insurance costs:

CategoryReference Premium (Annual)
AdultsCHF 4'640 / year
Young Adults (in education)CHF 3'446 / year
Children (0-18)CHF 1'034 / year
3

Deductible & Minimum Guarantee

First, the variable rate is determined:

If LNA > 45,000:
Rate = 7% + ((LNA - 45,000) / 1,000 * 0.125%) (max. 12%)

Then, the minimum guarantee is checked (only if LNA ≤ CHF 75,000):

CategoryGuaranteed Portion
Children80% of the reference premium
Youth in education50% of the reference premium
4

Final Entitlement

Entitlement = Max([Reference Premiums - Deductible], [Minimum Guarantee])

The amount is capped by the actual premium and must be at least CHF 100.

Special feature in the Canton of AI:
Unlike many other cantons, Appenzell Innerrhoden uses a linear progression for the deductible rate between CHF 45,000 and CHF 85,000, enabling a very equitable distribution of costs.
