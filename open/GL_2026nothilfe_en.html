Trust is based on facts. In the Canton of Glarus (GL), emergency aid for persons required to leave Switzerland is calculated using a model that combines cantonal rates with federal bonus parameters. Here we disclose the complete algorithm implemented in our system for 2026.

1. Calculation Parameters (GL 2026)

Base Daily Rate (Adult)

CHF 9.00 / day

Basic needs for a single person in collective accommodation.

Couple Factor (Additional adult)

CHF 15.30 / day (couple)

Second person receives 70% surcharge on base rate (total factor 1.70).

Child Supplement (55%)

CHF 4.95 / day per child

Calculated as 55% of the base rate of CHF 9.00.

2. Calculation Process (Step by Step)

1

Determine the Adult Daily Rate

The Canton of Glarus applies a degressive scale for couples and multi-person households:

1 adult: CHF 9.00 / day
2 adults (couple): CHF 15.30 / day
From 3 adults: 15.30 + (number - 2) × 9.00 × 0.70
2

Child Supplement & Large Family Bonus

In addition to the basic child rate (CHF 4.95), a federal bonus coefficient of 0.10 is applied from the 3rd child onward:

Child amount = (number of children × 4.95) + IF(children ≥ 3; (children - 2) × 0.90; 0)

The bonus adds CHF 0.90 per day for each child beyond the second.

3

Monthly Extrapolation & Rounding

The total is extrapolated to 30.42 days and rounded commercially to the nearest 5 centimes (CHF 0.05).

Monthly entitlement = ROUND( (Daily total × 30.42) ; 0.05 )
Special features in Canton GL:
• Accommodation: The calculation is based on placement in cantonal collective housing.
• In-kind benefits priority: As throughout Switzerland, emergency aid is primarily provided through in-kind benefits (accommodation, meals).
• Data status: Based on the cantonal ordinance on emergency aid, as of January 2026.
