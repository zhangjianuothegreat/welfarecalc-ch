2. The Calculation Path

1

Relevant Income (LNA)

The LNA is determined very precisely in the Canton of GL:

Net income + 10% (Assets - Allowance*) + Adjustments** - Child deductions

*Allowance: CHF 50,000 (single persons) / CHF 100,000 (married couples).
**Adjustments: Including imputed rental value, mortgage interest, and maintenance payments.
