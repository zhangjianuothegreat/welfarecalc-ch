📢 About this Dental Blog Series
This matrix page consolidates six independent guides written for residents and newcomers in Switzerland. Each article contains practical examples, DENTOTAR code lookups, and actionable checklists. The content reflects the legal framework of the Swiss dental tariff (DENTOTAR®) and real market data for 2026.

💡 Quick tip: To compare dentist prices, always ask for the clinic’s Taxpunktwert (TPW) before scheduling major work — a difference of 0.20 CHF per point can save hundreds of francs on larger treatments.
